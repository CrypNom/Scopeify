Frequently asked

Pricing questions, answered directly.

Can we start on Deployment Framework and move to Continuous Scale Hub?

Yes. Migration happens at any renewal date and carries over existing infrastructure and brand assets — nothing is rebuilt.

Are the four subsystems billed separately?

No. Every tier includes all four subsystems under one contract, one invoice, and one SLA.

What happens if we exceed a tier's included capacity?

Additional container nodes, ad platforms, or collateral requests are quoted transparently before any overage is billed.

Is there a minimum contract term?

Deployment Framework runs on a 6-month minimum term; Continuous Scale Hub runs month-to-month after an initial 3-month ramp.

Not sure which tier fits your current stack?

A systems strategist will scope your requirements against both tiers in one session — no obligation.
